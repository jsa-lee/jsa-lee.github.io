dating apps research

I worked with Prof. Roderic Crooks from the UCI Department of Informatics to write a paper on the interface of commonly used dating apps. During my tenure as an undergraduate researcher, I created profiles on twelve of the most commonly used dating apps in 2019 and collected data on the information requested of new users. I also performed preliminary market research on the online dating service market, and created schemas to code the collected data in an organized way.

The paper is set to be published in ACM CHI 2020 proceedings with the title "Determining the Extractive Casting Mold of Intimate Platforms through Document Theory".

Role Undergraduate researcher
Duration January to June 2019 (two 10-week quarters)
Methods and tools Literature review, interface analysis, market research
