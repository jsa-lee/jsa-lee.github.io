I'm a UX researcher passionate about games, emerging technologies, and the communities surrounding them.


I'm interested in how human biases influence emerging technologies, and in turn, how those technologies change us in return. My dream is to discover and create user experiences that change the lives of our most vulnerable peers for the better.

My strengths as a UX researcher include conducting many kinds of evaluations in order to acquire design insights from an expert perspective. I also excel at surveying and interviewing target audiences to find what parts of software matter to them the most. Subsequently, I use those insights to inform my UX design process, and the results of my research show in my ideas, wireframes, and prototypes.

Outside of UX, my interests are varied. I've worked in esports, organized hackathons, and even played competitive quidditch! I'm also interested in game dev; I designed a casual card game and am currently designing my own TTRPG campaign. Feel free to talk to me about anything here!
