I’m a UX designer, researcher, and project manager who’s passionate about games and social computing. My dream is to work in games and make an experience that can touch the hearts of players all over the world.

Over the course of my undergrad career, I’ve been the marketing manager for Anteater Quidditch, written for UCI Esports, and researched dating apps with Prof. Roderic Crooks. I currently give back to my community by serving as co-president of ICS Student Council and on the marketing board at Hack at UCI.

Outside of my work, I like reading cultural studies papers, playing Smash Ultimate (Isabelle, Bowser, and K. Rool main), and exploring different fictional universes! If you’d like to connect with me, you can find me on social media or email me at the links below.
